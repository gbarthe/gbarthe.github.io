Gilles Barthe

I am scientific director at Max Planck Institute for Security and Privacy, Bochum, Germany and (part-time) research professor at IMDEA Software Institute, Madrid, Spain. My research interests include programming languages and program verification, software and system security, cryptography, privacy, and mathematical logic. My main current interests are (relational) program verification and high-assurance cryptography. Check out the Formosa project.
